About me

Hi, I'm Yu, a software engineer working in the self-driving industry. In my spare time you can usually find me climbing, reading or creating some kind of art. This blog serves as a medium for me to document and share my ideas.

Why the name?

Many of my friends call me by fish because my name has the same pronunciation as "fish" in Chinese. One day I bumped into a video on youtube titled "my room becomes the sea". It resonates with me really well as I can see myself floating in the sea of memories lying on my bed every night before I go to sleep for a daily reflection. To me, each memory is similar to a small world behind the glasses in the aquarium. I can feel them, but not touch them. I can visit them multiple times a day, but I can never get inside. I can even share with friends and invite them to take a look, which is precisely what I hope this blog can serve as: a medium for me to record and share my ideas and experiences.
